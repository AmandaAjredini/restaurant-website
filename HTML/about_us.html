More About us and our Credibility

We believe that Prime Irish Beef is the Best in the World and have teamed up with Doyle's of Dublin - Family Master Butchers to offer some Fantastic Prime Cut Irish Steaks for your enjoyment.

All of our Beef is Family Farm Prime Irish, fully traceable from Farm to Fork and Dry Aged for at least 28 days for maximum Taste & Flavour.

Our fruit and vegetables come from a very reuptable local Irish company Vernon Catering ltd. ensuring the extra freshness of our produce.

Our cheese and pasta come from a well-known and trusted company K&C Norton.
